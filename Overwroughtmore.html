This was my first full project in Unreal Engine

I used Blue prints to program this project

This is a group project that is being made with a group of six

I am the main programmer for this project and did a majority of the in-engine development.

game concept follows you, the player, in your mind, dealing with past trauma by going to emotional states in your head. The main selling point of our game is the Narative-based puzzles in the game that the player has to complete in order to unlock items which trigger different events in your life.

Every event tells a story, and at every level it’s own intricate puzzle to go along with the vibe of every level.

Genre: 3D First Person, Narrative-Puzzle Game.
